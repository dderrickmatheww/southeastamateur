About the league

The Southeast Amateur League is a competitive video game league. We aim to become a stepping stone for kids, teenagers, and adults to becoming professional eSport players. Our goal is to provide a platform to anyone that would like to compete in a 'eSport-like' environment. Have you ever wondered how it would feel to compete in your favorite games for prizes? What about with your friends? Create your team, compete againsts other teams, and see if you can take home cash, prizes, or even the S.A.L. (Southeast Amateur League) trophy.


The league is still under developement but we will still be hosting stand alone tournaments for teams to compete for cash and prizes. This will eventually turn into the S. A. L.

If you are interested in competeing in this tournaments click the compete tab above and fill out the information.
